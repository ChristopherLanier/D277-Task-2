History of Ohio

Ohio, part of the Northwest Territory in the 1800s, quickly became known for being progressive and a major industrial state. Jobs were plentiful as industries scaled creating opportunities for hundreds of thousands of immigrants in addition to those migrating from appalachia and the deep south. Today Ohio is disverse with a mixture of different cultures and traditions.
